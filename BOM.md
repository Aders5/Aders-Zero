# Bill of Materials (BOM) - Aders-Zero

MCU: ESP32-S3 Lonely Binary Gold Edition

RF Module: CC1101 (Sub-GHz 433MHz)

NFC Module: PN532 (I2C Interface)

Display: 2.0" TFT LCD (Driver: ST7789V)

IR System (Receiver): TSOP38238 (38kHz Infrared Receiver)

IR System (Emitter): 940nm IR LED with 2N2222 Driver Circuit

Charging Module: TP4056 

Storage: MicroSD Card Reader Module (SPI Interface)

Power Source: 3.7V Li-Po Battery (2000mAh)

Input Control: EC11 Rotary Encoder

Wiring (Power): 26 AWG Silicone Wire

Wiring (Data): 30 AWG Silicone Wire
